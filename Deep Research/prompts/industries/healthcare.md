## Signal Reference (Comprehensive)

### FDA Warning Letter
**What it is:** FDA enforcement requiring compliance remediation
**Keywords to detect:** "FDA warning letter", "Form 483", "untitled letter", "regulatory action", "inspection findings"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Specific 483 observations and warning letter citations
- Facility type and products affected
- Response deadline and corrective action requirements
- Prior warning history and regulatory standing
- Import alert or injunction risk
- Remediation scope and third-party assessment needs

---

### Phase III Trial Initiated
**What it is:** Large-scale clinical operations creating consulting needs
**Keywords to detect:** "Phase III", "pivotal trial", "registrational study", "enrollment", "first patient dosed"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Trial design and patient population size
- Number of sites and geographic scope
- Primary and secondary endpoints
- Estimated completion timeline
- Sponsor capabilities and outsourcing model
- Regulatory submission strategy (NDA, BLA, MAA)

---

### CMS Procurement / RFP
**What it is:** Government healthcare contract opportunity
**Keywords to detect:** "CMS RFP", "Medicare procurement", "quality program", "CMMI", "CMS contract"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Solicitation details and response deadline
- Program scope and performance requirements
- Incumbent contractor and contract history
- Evaluation criteria and small business goals
- Technical requirements and staffing
- Period of performance and funded value

---

### HIPAA Breach
**What it is:** Privacy/security incident requiring remediation
**Keywords to detect:** "HIPAA breach", "OCR settlement", "protected health information", "data breach", "PHI exposure"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Number of individuals affected
- Root cause and attack vector
- OCR investigation status and settlement terms
- State AG involvement and class action exposure
- Required corrective action plan
- Security program remediation needs

---

### FDA Approval
**What it is:** Product approval creating post-market surveillance needs
**Keywords to detect:** "FDA approval", "510(k) clearance", "PMA approval", "breakthrough designation", "NDA approved"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Approval pathway and conditions
- Post-market commitment requirements (PMCs, PMRs)
- REMS program requirements
- Pharmacovigilance and safety reporting obligations
- Manufacturing scale-up challenges
- Commercial launch readiness

---

### GxP Compliance Issues
**What it is:** Quality system deficiencies requiring remediation
**Keywords to detect:** "GCP", "GLP", "GMP", "CAPA", "deviation", "quality system", "data integrity"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Specific GxP domain affected (clinical, lab, manufacturing)
- Nature of quality system gaps
- Regulatory inspection history
- CAPA backlog and cycle time issues
- Data integrity concerns
- Validation and qualification gaps

---

### Clinical Hold
**What it is:** Trial suspension requiring resolution
**Keywords to detect:** "clinical hold", "partial clinical hold", "IND hold", "trial suspension"
**What to research when this signal is requested:**
- Reason for hold (safety, CMC, protocol)
- Products and trials affected
- FDA correspondence and response requirements
- Timeline for hold resolution
- Impact on development program
- Sponsor remediation approach
